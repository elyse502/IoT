# Other Lessons
The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to a network of physical "things"—devices, appliances, and sensors—embedded with technology to connect and exchange data over the internet. These smart devices monitor, collect data, and often act on it, enabling automation and improved efficiency in homes, industries, and cities. 👉 [REF](https://www.oracle.com/uk/internet-of-things/#:~:text=The%20Internet%20of%20Things%20(IoT)%20describes%20the,ordinary%20household%20objects%20to%20sophisticated%20industrial%20tools.)
